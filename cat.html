Repositories & Catalogues

Requirements on Data Repositories & Catalogues

"new governance structures must be able to address the concerns of research participants while at the same time ensuring effective data sharing that promotes public trust in genomics research." (Kaye, 2012)

"data curation services will need to accommodate a wide range of subdisciplinary data characteristics and sharing practices." (Cragin, 2010)

Repository & Catalogue Exemplars

  • Scientific Data, launched by Nature Research to enable the discoverability, reproducibility and reuse of valuable data.
  • CKAN, the world’s leading open-source data portal platform.
  • UniProt, a comprehensive, high-quality and freely accessible resource of protein sequence and functional information.
  • Web Services for the Virtual Observatory (Szalay et al., 2002)
  • ORCHESTRA (Green et al., 2007)
  • Web Observatory (Tinati, Wang, Tiropanis & Hall, 2015), the best of all :P
