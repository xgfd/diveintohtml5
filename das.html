Data Access Requirements

A Data Access/Availability Statement is required by many publications, research funders and universities. It provides 1) where data directly supporting the publication can be found and 2) under what conditions they can be accessed. A Data Access Statement generator is already available for Latex.

The Science family of journals is committed to sharing data relevant to public health emergencies.

Nature: "Supporting data must be made available to editors and peer reviewers at the time of submission for the purposes of evaluating the manuscript."
