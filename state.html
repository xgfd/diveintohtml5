The State of the Art

According to a survey on the first 10 original research papers of 2009 published in the 50 original research journals with the highest impact factor, only 47 papers (9%) deposited full primary raw data online, and 149 (30%) were not subject to any data availability policy (Alsheikh-Ali et al., 2011).

In the long tail of science and technology "data are not readily discoverable because investigators do not contribute them to repositories, accompanied by metadata and documentation." (Wallis et al., 2013)

Un-willingness to publish data is proportional to:

(data management + legal/policy acknowledgement) * amount of data

(Sayogo & Pardo, 2013)
