For & Aginst Sharing Scientific Data

No

  • time consuming/
    insufficient time (Tenopir et al., 2011)
  • requirement on data management skills
  • hosting data is not free
  • lack of funding (Tenopir et al., 2011)
  • legal issues (Kuipers et al., 2009)
  • data misuse (Kuipers et al., 2009)
  • lack of support
  • technical obstacles (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • documentation/
    curation (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • confidentiality/privacy
  • another factor to be rejected

Yes

  • requirement by various organisations
  • reinforcement of open scientific inquire (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • verification of results (some authors may consider it in the no category) (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • refinement of original results (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • promotion of new research through existing data (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • protection against faulty data (Fienberg et al., 1985)
  • citation increase (Piwowar et al., 2007)
